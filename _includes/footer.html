About the Host

I’m Tim Smith, a designer and frontend developer from Saint Paul, MN. I’ve worked on the web for a decade, working with different companies and clients. I love passing on knowledge by speaking and giving workshops. In 2013, I created The Bold Report, a blog about design, development, technology, and most importantly, Star Wars. When away from my desk, I spend time with my amazing wife Kelly, eating brunch, watching movies, walking the mall, and other sappy-sounding couple stuff.
